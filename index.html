The Matrix

I just recently caught The Matrix on TV and it took up my whole afternoon. This inspired me to create a cool design using different elements from the movie. I used two different images and blended them to create a falling code effect.

Black and White

This exhibit was so fun to make! I took an in-game photo from the video game Ghost of Tsushima and I was able to edit it in photoshop to decolorize it and make it black and white. I got the idea because in the game there is a mode that makes the gameplay black and white to honor old samurai films.

New Years Card

For this exhibit, I wanted to use some gradients along with adjustment layers for color. I decided to make a New Year's card. I wanted something colorful but also something that utilized some contrast.

Game of Thrones

For this Exhibit I wanted to page homage to one of my favorite series Game of Thrones. With this design I wanted to use colors and text to go along with the theme of Winterfell which is very cold and rustic, with the house symbols of the Starks being a wolf.
